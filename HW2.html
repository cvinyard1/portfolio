The Best Movies According to Camden

My Top 3 Movies

Cars

I have loved this movie ever since I was roughly three or four. It was one of the first movies I had ever seen, and I beleive that the original is alwayas going to be a clasic. I haven't seen it in years but I'd be doing it a diservice by not including it in my list.

Blade Runner 2049

I watched this movie about three years ago, and fell in love with it. The art design is unbelievable, and not only with the studio props, but also the sets themselves, and the camera angles.

Stand By Me

Stand By Me is such an all time classic, that I wasn't even alive to see in theatres. Anytime that I watch that movie I wish that I was born in the time frame that the movie is set in. When I saw the movie as a kid, I believe that it made me want to go on more hikes, and exploe in the woods more.
